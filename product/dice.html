How It Works

Use the guide that comes with your dice to arrange them in a color wheel to explore color harmonies like complements, triads, tetrads, and more. Pick your dice and give them a roll, and use the handy hex codes for easy digitization.

Each die represents one hue, like red. On the red die, the 6 sides are combinations of saturations (brightness to dullness) and values (darkness to lightness). On any die, there are light, dark, and medium values that are saturated (pure), and light, dark, and medium values that are desaturated (grayer).

If you need a light or dark color, flip a die until you find it. Use your roll as a starting point, or let the dice choose your fate.

Use the hex codes on each face to use your new color set in software. Enjoy!
